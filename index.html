🌊 The Ocean Needs Our Help!

Pollution and storms break our sensors. When sensors break, the AI goes "blind" — it can't see the animals in danger! 🐢 That's why Rookie Engineers like you are so important!

🤖 AI Learns Like You Do!

AI looks at thousands of photos of healthy oceans and sick oceans. Then it remembers the difference — just like you learned to tell apart a cat 🐱 and a dog 🐕!

📊 Data = Clues!

Every piece of ocean data is like a clue 🔍. When the AI collects many clues, it can solve the mystery — just like we solved today's mystery about the missing sea creatures! 🐬
